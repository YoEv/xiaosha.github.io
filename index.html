Hi, this is Xiaosha Li (黎小莎). I’m a researcher at Georgia Tech working on audio representation learning, music-AI evaluation, and large language–music models. I’m currently advised by Alex Lerch and collaborate with Ziyu Wang and Chun Liu. Previously I studied at Berklee College of Music and have worked across composition, audio ML, and interactive media. My current projects explore noise-perturbation evaluation for generative music systems, token-level analysis of loss, and practical tools that help musicians interact with AI models.
